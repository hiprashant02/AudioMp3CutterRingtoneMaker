Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Audio Editor ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our MP3 Cutter & Ringtone Maker application.

Information We Access

Our app requires access to the following device features to function properly:

  • Audio Files: We access audio files stored on your device through the MediaStore API to allow you to select, edit, cut, merge, and convert audio files.
  • Storage: We read audio files from your device storage and save exported files to your Music folder.
  • Microphone: If you use the voice recorder feature, we access your device's microphone to record audio.
  • System Settings: If you choose to set a ringtone, we request permission to modify system settings.

Data Collection

We do NOT collect any personal data.

Audio Editor operates entirely offline. All audio processing happens locally on your device. We do not:

  • Upload your audio files to any server
  • Collect usage analytics or tracking data
  • Access your contacts, location, or other personal information
  • Share any data with third parties

Data Storage

All data remains on your device. We store:

  • Exported audio files in your device's Music folder
  • App preferences locally on your device

We do not have access to any cloud storage or remote servers.

Third-Party Services

Audio Editor does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app functions completely independently.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any data and is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app with an updated "Last updated" date.
